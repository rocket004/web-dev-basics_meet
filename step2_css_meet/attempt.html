Space Situational Awareness & Debris Resource

Idea:

This is my first attempt to share various links to an accurate and efficient core layers for building space flight dynamics applications.

Description:

These links aims at providing accurate and efficient low level components for the development of flight dynamics applications. It is designed to be easily used in very different contexts, from quick studies up to critical operations. Libraries provides basic elements (orbits, dates, attitude, frames, ...) and various algorithms to handle them (conversions, propagations, pointing, ...)
